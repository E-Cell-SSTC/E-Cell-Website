March 2019

Awareness drive on Oil and Gas Start-up initiative

On 31 st march 2019 E-CELL SSTC Bhilai organized anawareness drive On “Oil And Gas Start up initiative “.The event was sponsored by KIIT Technology Business Incubator (KIIT-TBI).

KIIT TBI (Technical Business Incubator, Bhubaneswar) is a well-recognised scientific and industrial research organization by DSIR and one of the leading and prominent incubator cells of nation. In order to promote entrepreneurship and to boost innovation KIIT TBI being the implementing partner along with renowned Oil and Gas Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) is providing a holistic platform to the budding entrepreneurs and innovative brains. It is providing a common portal for short listing of start-ups for funding (Rs. 5-100Cr to individual start-ups) by Oil and Gas PSUs to engage in incubation support/consultancy services.
